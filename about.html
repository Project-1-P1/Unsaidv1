UNSAID

Documentation & Philosophy

UNSAID is an ephemeral communication system built for honesty.

In a digital world that records every click, creates permanent profiles, and ranks us by engagement, silence often feels safer than expression. We curate our lives because we know the internet never forgets.

UNSAID removes the memory. It removes the profile. It removes the metrics.

What remains is a pure stream of thoughtâ€”text that exists for a chosen moment and then vanishes completely. It is designed to be a digital "safe space" where you can speak without the weight of identity attached to your words.

How It Works

The interface is minimal by design, but dense with utility. Here is how to navigate the system.

Fluid Identity

You don't have an account. When you open the site, you are assigned a random Alias (e.g., Neon-Signal-9921). This alias lasts until you close your tab or refresh. Next time, you will be someone new.

Swipe to Reply

To keep the interface clean, reply buttons are secondary. Simply swipe right on any message bubble to instantly quote it and start a reply.

Spaces

Conversations happen in Spaces. The Global Feed is public to everyone. You can create or join specific topics (e.g., #music, #vent) using the search bar.

Time Limits

Before sending, tap the timer pill (24h, 3 Days, 7 Days). This is the lifespan of your message. Once it expires, it is physically deleted from the database.

Deep Threading

Unlike standard chat apps, UNSAID supports infinite threading. You can reply to a reply to a reply. Clicking "View Thread" isolates that specific conversation branch, allowing for focused discussions without the noise of the main feed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Am I truly anonymous? Yes. We do not require an email, phone number, or password. We do not log IP addresses for tracking purposes. Your "Alias" is generated locally on your device and resets every session.
Can I get my old alias back? No. Once you close your tab or clear your cache, that identity is lost forever. This is intentional to prevent long-term tracking or reputation building.
Where do messages go when they expire? Nowhere. They are not "archived" or "hidden." Our database runs a cleanup function that physically overwrites and deletes the row. They are gone.
Why can't I edit my message? UNSAID captures a moment in time. Once said, it stands. If you regret it, you must wait for it to fade away, or you can report it for removal if it violates terms.
Can I create a private space? Currently, all Spaces are public. If you create a Space with a complex name (e.g., #x99-secret-club), it acts as a semi-private room, but anyone who guesses the name can enter.
Is this project finished? UNSAID is an experimental initiative. It is "finished" in its core philosophy, but we may refine the experience based on how people use it.
